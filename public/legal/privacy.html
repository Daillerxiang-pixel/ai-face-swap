Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Welcome to AI Face Swap! We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, nickname, and avatar provided during registration
  • Usage Data: App usage records, feature usage frequency, operation logs
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifier
  • Photo Content: Photos you upload for face swapping (used only for generating results, automatically deleted after processing)

2. Face Data Collection, Use, and Retention

Because our app provides AI-powered face swapping, we want to be transparent about how we handle face data specifically:

2.1 What face data we collect: When you upload a photo for face swapping, our AI service detects and extracts facial geometry (landmarks, contours, and positioning data) from the photo in real time solely for the purpose of generating the face swap result. We do not create, store, or build any persistent facial recognition templates, biometric identifiers, or face-print databases.

2.2 How we use face data: Face data is used exclusively for generating the face swap output image that you requested. The facial geometry data exists only in volatile memory during the processing step and is discarded as soon as the result image is produced. We do not use face data for identification, authentication, advertising, analytics, or any purpose other than producing the face swap result you initiated.

2.3 Third-party sharing: To perform the face swap, the uploaded photo is sent to Tencent Cloud AI (Face Fusion API) via encrypted HTTPS. Tencent Cloud processes the image in real time and returns the result. Per Tencent Cloud's data processing terms, they do not retain or use customer-submitted images beyond the scope of the API call. No face data is shared with any other third party. The uploaded image is temporarily stored on Alibaba Cloud OSS solely to relay it to the processing API.

2.4 Storage location: Uploaded photos and generated results are stored temporarily on Alibaba Cloud OSS servers. Face geometry data is not stored persistently — it exists only in transient processing memory on Tencent Cloud's API servers during the API call.

2.5 Retention period: All uploaded source photos and generated result images are automatically deleted within 24 hours after processing. No face data, facial geometry, or biometric information is retained beyond the duration of the individual API processing call (typically a few seconds).

Important Commitment: Your uploaded photos are used only for generating face swap results. They are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours after processing. Face geometry data is never persisted. We will not use your photos or face data for any other purpose or share them with third parties beyond what is described above.
